About me

Hello! I'm Taoufik Oujaa, a 25-year-old passionate programmer with a strong background in applied mathematics (DEUG). Currently, I’m diving deep into web development and enhancing my skills with a focus on creating intuitive, responsive websites and mobile applications.

With a love for programming and a curiosity about computer science, I constantly stay updated with the latest developments in Artificial Intelligence (AI). I’m particularly fascinated by large language models (LLMs) and the rapid advancements in the field, always keeping an eye on the top-rated models and their impact on various industries.

In terms of my skills, I’m proficient in Python, HTML, and Bootstrap—technologies I’ve been actively learning and using to build functional and visually appealing projects. I am continuously improving and pushing myself to master new frameworks and tools that will enhance my development skills.

My ultimate goal is to either work as a freelance developer or join a dynamic company as a web/mobile app developer. I’m eager to contribute to meaningful projects, collaborate with talented teams, and grow my expertise in both development and AI.

Feel free to browse my projects and reach out if you’d like to collaborate or chat about web development, AI, or the latest in tech!
